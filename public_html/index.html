About Us

SpotifAI is an initiative for sports people who are suffering from mental health issues ranging from anxiety and depression, to obsessive behaviours and eating disorders.

Browse through the menu in the top right corner to learn more about common mental health disorders, and coping mechanisms you can practice. However, please note that this website is not a replacement for professional help.

Sometimes, regardless of your problem, you just need a sympathetic ear. By clicking on the SportBot tab, you will be redirected to SportifAI, an AI bot on Facebook Messenger, who will listen to you rant, and if you are a sportsperson, can offer advice and resources for any mental health challenges you may be facing.

About the Founder

Nitya Khanna is the founder and creator of SportifAI. She is a high school senior at Delhi Public School R. K. Puram, India. She has always been fascinated with psychology and computer science, and wants to major in Cognitive Science at college. She created SportifAI as platform to educate people on mental health and how to cope with psychological disorders, as well as to help people feel less alone in their struggles.
